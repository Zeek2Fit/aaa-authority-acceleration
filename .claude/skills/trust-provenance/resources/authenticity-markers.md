# Authenticity Markers

What makes provenance content genuine:
- Specific dates and timelines
- Named sources and influences
- Admission of what you don't know
- Evolution of thinking shown
- Real examples from your journey
- Imperfect moments included
- Credits given to others

## Authenticity Checklist

Before publishing provenance content, verify:
- [ ] Includes specific dates/timelines
- [ ] Credits influences by name
- [ ] Admits limitations/unknowns
- [ ] Shows evolution (not static)
- [ ] Includes real examples
- [ ] Has imperfect moments
- [ ] Feels genuinely YOU
