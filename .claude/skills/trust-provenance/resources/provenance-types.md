# Provenance Types

## 1. RESEARCH METHODOLOGY
**Pattern:** "How I research X"
**Shows:** Intellectual rigor
**Trust Impact:** High (proves depth)

## 2. ORIGIN STORY
**Pattern:** "How I developed X framework/approach"
**Shows:** Authentic development journey
**Trust Impact:** Very High (personal + professional)

## 3. INFLUENCES & SOURCES
**Pattern:** "Who/what shaped my thinking"
**Shows:** Intellectual honesty, give credit
**Trust Impact:** High (builds community)

## 4. ITERATION HISTORY
**Pattern:** "How this evolved over time"
**Shows:** Continuous improvement
**Trust Impact:** High (not static/dogmatic)

## 5. CREATION PROCESS
**Pattern:** "How I create X content"
**Shows:** Behind-the-scenes work
**Trust Impact:** Medium-High (demystifies)

## 6. MISTAKE CATALOG
**Pattern:** "What I used to believe vs. now"
**Shows:** Growth, intellectual honesty
**Trust Impact:** Very High (vulnerability)
