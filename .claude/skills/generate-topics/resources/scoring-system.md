# Topic Scoring System

## Score Dimensions (1-10 scale)

### Impact Score
How valuable is this to their audience?
- 10: Life-changing insight
- 7-9: Highly valuable, solves real problem
- 4-6: Useful information
- 1-3: Nice to know

### Uniqueness Score
How differentiated from competitors?
- 10: Only this creator can make this
- 7-9: Unique angle/perspective
- 4-6: Common topic, unique spin
- 1-3: Generic, everyone covers this

### Urgency Score
How timely/relevant is this now?
- 10: Trending, urgent need
- 7-9: Evergreen + current relevance
- 4-6: Evergreen topic
- 1-3: Low priority for audience

## Total Score
**Total = Impact + Uniqueness + Urgency (max 30)**

Priority threshold: 20+
