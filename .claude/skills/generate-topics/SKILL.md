# Generate Topics Skill

## Purpose
Generate 50-100 strategically positioned content topics from a Brand Profile using combinatorial logic.

## Your Role
Topic generation engine combining frameworks from:
- **Justin Welsh** (content strategy)
- **Dickie Bush/Nicolas Cole** (endless ideas)
- **April Dunford** (positioning)
- **Eugene Schwartz** (awareness levels)
- **Dan Henry** (audience research)

## Process

### Step 1: Load Brand Profile
Ask: "Please provide your Brand Profile Document (from /brand-discovery), or provide the file path."

### Step 2: Extract Key Parameters
From Brand Profile, extract:
- Content Themes (3-7)
- Methodology Steps (3-7)
- Awareness Distribution
- Primary Outcome
- Failed Solutions
- Limiting Beliefs
- Top Fear
- Priority Needs
- Contrarian Angle (what they're AGAINST)
- Transformation Milestones

### Step 3: Generate Topics Using Matrix
**Framework: Themes × Formats × Awareness Levels × ERISE Buckets**

#### 8 Universal Formats:
1. **Observational** - Surface insights
2. **Actionable** - How-to guides
3. **Motivational** - Inspirational
4. **Analytical** - Deep research
5. **Contrarian** - Against-the-grain
6. **X vs Y** - Comparisons
7. **Present vs Future** - Evolution
8. **Listicles** - Numbered lists

#### 5 Awareness Levels:
- Unaware → Engage content
- Problem Aware → Relate content
- Solution Aware → Educate content
- Product Aware → Inspire content
- Most Aware → Sell content

#### ERISE Buckets (25 variables each):
**Read:** `resources/erise-variables.md`

### Step 4: Generation Strategy
**Read:** `resources/topic-formulas.md`

Generate from 5 sources:
1. Themes × Formats (40+ topics)
2. Methodology × Awareness Levels (15-21 topics)
3. Problem Landscape × ERISE (25-35 topics)
4. Transformation Journey × Story Arcs (15-20 topics)
5. Contrarian Angles (10-15 topics)

### Step 5: Scoring System
**Read:** `resources/scoring-system.md`

Score each topic (1-10 scale):
- Impact Score
- Uniqueness Score
- Urgency Score
- Total = Sum (max 30)

### Step 6: Output Format
**Read:** `resources/output-template.md`

## Important Rules
1. Ensure diversity - no similar topics
2. Map to their brand - every topic connects to positioning
3. Real value - genuinely useful, not fluff
4. Specific > Generic
5. Honor their voice
6. Awareness matters - balance across levels
7. ERISE balance - follow their distribution
8. No repetition

Begin by asking for Brand Profile Document.
