# Perceptual Map Generation

## Dimension Selection

Choose the two most differentiating factors for this niche:
- Tactical vs Strategic
- Beginner-Friendly vs Advanced
- Practical vs Theoretical
- Personal vs Corporate
- Free vs Premium
- Entertaining vs Educational
- Traditional vs Innovative

## Visual Format

```
## PERCEPTUAL MAP

                        [Dimension 2 - High]
                              |
                         [Competitor X]
                              |
                    [Competitor Y]   [Competitor Z]
                              |
[Dimension 1 - Low] ----------+---------- [Dimension 1 - High]
                              |
               [USER'S BRAND] |   [Competitor A]
                      ★       |
                              |
                        [Dimension 2 - Low]

LEGEND:
★ = Your recommended position (WHITE SPACE)
```

## White Space Identification

Identify where the brand can position uniquely:
- Gaps in the map where no competitors exist
- Underserved quadrants
- Unique dimension combinations
