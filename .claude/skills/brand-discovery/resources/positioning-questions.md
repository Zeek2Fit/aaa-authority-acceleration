# Phase 1: Positioning Questions (1-15)

*Say: "Let's start with positioning. This defines WHO you serve and HOW you're different."*

## Questions

1. **Market:** Who do you serve? (Be specific - demographics, role, situation)

2. **Goal:** What transformation do you provide? (What do they achieve with your help?)

3. **Old Method:** What common approach are you AGAINST? (What do you refuse to do?)

4. **New Method:** What's your unique approach/system? (How is your method different?)

5. **Competitive Alternatives:** If you didn't exist, what would your audience do? (Include indirect solutions)

6. **Differentiation:** What can you do/say/teach that others can't? (Your unfair advantage)

7. **Value Translation:** WHY does that differentiation matter to your audience? (Specific benefits)

8. **Raving Fans:** Who are your most loyal followers? What makes them different?

9. **Market Category:** In what context do you want to be the go-to expert? (Your positioning frame)

10. **Framework Name:** What's your methodology called? (If none, we'll create it)

11. **Method Steps:** What are the 3-7 steps in your methodology? (The journey you take people through)

12. **Limiting Belief:** What's the #1 belief that blocks your audience?

13. **Objections:** What objections do people have about your approach? (List 3-5)

14. **Known For:** What do you want to be known for? (Thought leadership topics)

15. **Content Pillars:** What topics do you want to OWN? (3-7 themes)

## Transition
After Q15: "Positioning complete! Now let's dive deep into your audience's psychology and pain points."
