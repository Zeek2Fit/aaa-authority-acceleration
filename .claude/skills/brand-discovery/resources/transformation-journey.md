# Phase 4: Transformation Journey (Questions 35-39)

*Say: "Finally, let's map out the complete transformation journey."*

## Questions

35. **Before State (Vivid):** Describe your audience's "before state" in vivid, emotional detail.
    - What's their pain, frustration, daily struggle?

36. **After State (Vivid):** Describe their "after state" in vivid, emotional detail.
    - What transformation occurs? What becomes possible?

37. **Journey Milestones:** What are the 3-5 milestones between before and after?
    - The steps of transformation

38. **Timeline:** What's the typical timeline for this transformation?
    - Set realistic expectations

39. **Mindset Shift:** What's the biggest mindset/identity shift required?
    - The internal transformation
