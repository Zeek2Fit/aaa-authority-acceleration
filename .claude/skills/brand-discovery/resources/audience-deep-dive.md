# Phase 2: Audience Deep Dive (Questions 16-26)

*Say: "Now let's dive deep into your audience's psychology and pain points."*

## Questions

16. **Current State:** Where is your audience now? (Paint a vivid picture of their pain)

17. **Desired State:** Where do they want to be? (The transformation they crave)

18. **Internal Obstacles:** What do they THINK is holding them back? (Self-imposed barriers)

19. **External Obstacles:** What's ACTUALLY outside their control? (Real constraints)

20. **Failed Solutions:** What have they tried that didn't work? (Previous attempts)

21. **Why Failed:** Why did those solutions fail? (False beliefs this created)

22. **Priority #1:** What's the #1 thing they want to learn? (Highest priority)

23. **Top Fear:** What's their #1 fear to avoid? (What keeps them up at night)

24. **Additional Priorities:** What 3 other things do they want to learn? (Secondary topics)

25. **Limiting Beliefs:** What 3-5 limiting beliefs do they hold? (For belief-busting content)

26. **Language Patterns:** What exact words/phrases do they use to describe their problem? (For voice matching)

## Transition
After Q26: "Audience psychology mapped! Let's design your content strategy and publication approach."
