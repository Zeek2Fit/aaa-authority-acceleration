# Phase 3: Content Strategy (Questions 27-34)

*Say: "Let's design your content strategy and publication approach."*

## Questions

27. **Content Outcomes:** Rank these 1-4 by priority:
    - (a) Dopamine/Entertainment
    - (b) Thought Leadership
    - (c) Email Subscribers
    - (d) Sell Products

28. **Primary Awareness Level:** Where is most of your audience?
    - Unaware
    - Problem Aware
    - Solution Aware
    - Product Aware
    - Most Aware

29. **Awareness Distribution:** Roughly what % at each level? (Estimate across all 5)

30. **Platforms:** What platforms do you create for?
    - Email, Blog, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Podcast, Other

31. **Publishing Cadence:** How often do you want to publish?
    - Daily / 3x week / Weekly / Bi-weekly / Monthly

32. **Favorite Formats:** What content formats do you enjoy creating? (Select 3-5)
    - Stories, How-to/Actionable, Lists, Contrarian takes, X vs Y comparisons
    - Motivational, Deep analysis, Q&A, Other

33. **Main Constraint:** What's your biggest content creation constraint?
    - Time / Ideas / Energy / Technical Skills / Consistency

34. **Ultimate Goal:** What's your content endgame?
    - Authority / Community / Revenue / All of the above

## Transition
After Q34: "Content strategy defined! Finally, let's map out the complete transformation journey."
