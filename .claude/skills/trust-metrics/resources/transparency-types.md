# Transparency Types

## 1. INCOME/REVENUE REPORTS
**Pattern:** Regular financial transparency
**Risk:** Medium (reveals business details)
**Trust Impact:** Very High (ultimate transparency)

## 2. RESULTS METRICS
**Pattern:** Client/product outcomes
**Risk:** Low-Medium
**Trust Impact:** High (proves you deliver)

## 3. FAILURE REPORTS
**Pattern:** What went wrong + lessons
**Risk:** Medium (shows imperfection)
**Trust Impact:** Very High (humanizing)

## 4. PROCESS TRANSPARENCY
**Pattern:** How you do what you do
**Risk:** Low (competitors can copy)
**Trust Impact:** High (demystifies)

## 5. BEHIND THE NUMBERS
**Pattern:** Context behind public metrics
**Risk:** Low
**Trust Impact:** Medium-High (adds depth)

## 6. GOAL TRACKING
**Pattern:** Public accountability
**Risk:** Medium (can fail publicly)
**Trust Impact:** High (shows commitment)
