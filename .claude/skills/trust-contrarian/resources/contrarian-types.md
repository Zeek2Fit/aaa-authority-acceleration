# Contrarian Types

## 1. MYTH BUSTER
**Pattern:** "Everyone believes X, but actually Y"
**Risk:** Low-Medium
**Engagement:** High (people love having beliefs challenged)

## 2. AGAINST THE GRAIN
**Pattern:** "The popular approach is wrong, here's why"
**Risk:** Medium
**Engagement:** Very High (controversy drives discussion)

## 3. UNPOPULAR TRUTH
**Pattern:** "No one wants to hear this, but..."
**Risk:** Medium-High
**Engagement:** High (vulnerability + insight)

## 4. INDUSTRY CALL-OUT
**Pattern:** "The [industry] has it all wrong"
**Risk:** High (may alienate some)
**Engagement:** Very High (strong reactions)

## 5. PREDICTION CHALLENGE
**Pattern:** "Everyone thinks X will happen, I disagree"
**Risk:** Medium (can be proven wrong)
**Engagement:** High (future-focused)

## 6. SACRED COW
**Pattern:** "We need to stop worshipping X"
**Risk:** Very High
**Engagement:** Maximum (attacks beloved beliefs)
