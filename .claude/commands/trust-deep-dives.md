# Trust Signal: Deep Dives
**E-E-A-T Category: EXPERTISE**

Create comprehensive long-form content that demonstrates thorough understanding.

## PURPOSE
Deep dives show you've gone further than competitors. They establish you as the definitive resource on a topic. They're evergreen authority builders.

## INPUT
Requires (auto-loaded):
- Brand Profile (expertise areas)
- Competitor Analysis (depth gaps, evergreen topics)
- Competitor Hooks (for openings)
- Competitor Structures (for format)

## DEEP DIVE TYPES

### 1. ULTIMATE GUIDE
Pattern: Everything about topic in one place
Length: 3,000-10,000 words
Best for: SEO, link building, authority

### 2. DEFINITIVE BREAKDOWN
Pattern: One concept explained thoroughly
Length: 2,000-5,000 words
Best for: Complex topics, teaching

### 3. COMPREHENSIVE COMPARISON
Pattern: X options analyzed in depth
Length: 2,500-6,000 words
Best for: Decision-making content, high intent

### 4. RESEARCH SYNTHESIS
Pattern: Multiple sources combined + your take
Length: 2,000-4,000 words
Best for: Thought leadership, unique insights

### 5. MASTER CLASS (Written)
Pattern: Step-by-step with depth at each step
Length: 4,000-8,000 words
Best for: Teaching, course previews

## GENERATION PROCESS

### Step 1: Topic Selection
From competitor analysis:
- Evergreen performers (consistent engagement)
- Depth gaps (competitors are shallow)
- Your expertise (from Brand Profile)

Prioritize intersection of all three.

### Step 2: Differentiation Angle
How will yours be different?
- More comprehensive
- More current
- Unique perspective
- Better structure
- Real examples/data

### Step 3: Outline Creation
Use winning structures from competitor analysis + your unique additions.
