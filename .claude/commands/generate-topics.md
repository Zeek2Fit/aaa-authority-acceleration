### Step 4: Generation Strategy

**Generate 50-100 topics using these sources:**

#### Source 1: Themes Ã— Formats (40+ topics)
For each theme, apply 5-8 formats most relevant to their content strategy.

**Example:**
- Theme: "Sustainable nutrition"
- Format: Contrarian
- Topic: "Calorie counting is sabotaging your results. Here's what actually works."

#### Source 2: Methodology Ã— Awareness Levels (15-21 topics)
For each methodology step, create topics for 3-5 awareness levels.

**Example:**
- Method Step: "Identity-first approach"
- Awareness: Problem Aware
- Topic: "Why diets fail but identity change lasts: The science of sustainable transformation"

#### Source 3: Problem Landscape Ã— ERISE (25-35 topics)
- Failed solutions â†’ Educate/Contrarian
- Limiting beliefs â†’ Inspire/Relate

- Priority needs â†’ Educate/Actionable

**Example:**
- Failed Solution: "Extreme diets"
- Bucket: Contrarian
- Topic: "I tried every extreme diet for 10 years. Here's why they all failed (and what finally worked)"

#### Source 4: Transformation Journey Ã— Story Arcs (15-20 topics)
For each milestone, create story-based content.

**Example:**
- Milestone: "First habit that sticks"
- Format: Motivational
- Topic: "The moment you realize discipline beats motivation: A transformation story"

#### Source 5: Contrarian Angles (10-15 topics)
Based on what they're AGAINST (their "old method").

**Example:**
- Against: "Hustle culture"
- Topic: "Hustle culture will burn you out. Here's the sustainable alternative that actually scales."

### Step 5: Scoring System

Score each topic on 3 dimensions (1-10 scale):

1. **Impact Score** - How valuable is this to their audience?
2. **Uniqueness Score** - How differentiated from competitors?
3. **Urgency Score** - How timely/relevant is this now?

**Total Score** = Impact + Uniqueness + Urgency (max 30)

Prioritize topics with scores 20+.

## IMPORTANT RULES

1. **Ensure diversity** - Don't generate 100 similar topics
2. **Map to their brand** - Every topic must connect to their positioning
3. **Real value** - Each topic should be genuinely useful, not fluff
4. **Specific > Generic** - "5 myths about intermittent fasting" not "fitness tips"
5. **Honor their voice** - Topic angles should match their style (get hints from brand profile)
6. **Awareness matters** - Balance topics across all 5 levels based on their distribution
7. **ERISE balance** - Follow their recommended ERISE distribution from brand profile
8. **No repetition** - Each topic must be distinct
