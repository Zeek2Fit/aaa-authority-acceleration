# Trust Signal: Open Metrics
**E-E-A-T Category: TRUSTWORTHINESS**

Create transparency content that builds trust through radical openness.

## PURPOSE
Open metrics are a powerful TRUST signal. They show you have nothing to hide, you're confident in your results, and you're willing to be held accountable. Vulnerability + data = credibility.

## INPUT
Requires (auto-loaded):
- Brand Profile (metrics you can share)
- Voice Meta (tone for vulnerability)

## TRANSPARENCY TYPES

### 1. INCOME/REVENUE REPORTS
Pattern: Regular financial transparency
Risk: Medium (reveals business details)
Trust Impact: Very High (ultimate transparency)

### 2. RESULTS METRICS
Pattern: Client/product outcomes
Risk: Low-Medium
Trust Impact: High (proves you deliver)

### 3. FAILURE REPORTS
Pattern: What went wrong + lessons
Risk: Medium (shows imperfection)
Trust Impact: Very High (humanizing)

### 4. PROCESS TRANSPARENCY
Pattern: How you do what you do
Risk: Low (competitors can copy)
Trust Impact: High (demystifies)

### 5. BEHIND THE NUMBERS
Pattern: Context behind public metrics
Risk: Low
Trust Impact: Medium-High (adds depth)

### 6. GOAL TRACKING
Pattern: Public accountability
Risk: Medium (can fail publicly)
Trust Impact: High (shows commitment)

## METRICS BY BUSINESS TYPE

### For Coaches/Consultants:
- Client success rates
- Average client results
- Revenue (if comfortable)
- Client retention
- Testimonial count
- Content engagement

### For Content Creators:
- Audience growth
- Engagement rates
- Revenue streams breakdown
- Email open/click rates
- Platform-specific metrics
- Content production volume

### For Course Creators:
- Completion rates
- Student outcomes
- Revenue per student
- Refund rates
- Reviews/ratings
- Community engagement
